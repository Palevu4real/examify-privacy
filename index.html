📱 Privacy Policy for Examify

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Examify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Examify.

Information We Collect

Personal Data: Examify does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • Quiz performance and scores (stored locally on your device)
  • Bookmarked questions (stored locally on your device)
  • App settings preferences (e.g., dark mode)

No Data Upload: All your quiz history, bookmarks, and settings are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Examify uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Examify is designed for students preparing for examinations, including minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All quiz data remains on the user's device.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since all your data is stored locally on your device, we recommend you protect your device with a password or biometric lock.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Delete bookmarks and history within the app's Settings menu
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
